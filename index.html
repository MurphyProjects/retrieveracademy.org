"Built by a teacher. Tested in the real world. Named after the best study buddy I ever had."

I'm a high school science teacher, hockey dad, and lifelong learner based in Ottawa. Retriever Academy started as a folder of resources I built for my own classroom — writing guides, planning templates, and finance spreadsheets built for real life.

The name comes from the breed that inspired it all. Labradors are land and water dogs — loyal, hardworking, always retrieving. That's what I try to bring to every tool I build. 🐾

Everything here is designed to save you time, not create more work. Self-explanatory out of the box — no post-purchase confusion.
